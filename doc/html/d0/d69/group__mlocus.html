Create a vector of callbacks bound to model interlocus recombination.

Example of a three locus system where loci 0 and 1 are 25cM apart and locus 2 is unlinked (50cM) from 0 and 1:

std::vector<double> recrates{0.25,0.5};
auto interlocus_rec =
KTfwd::make_binomial_interlocus_rec(r,recrates.data(),recrates.size());
